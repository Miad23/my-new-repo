Welcome to the Amsterdam Public Transport Guide, your complete resource for navigating the city’s transportation system. Whether you're here for a few days or longer, we’ll make sure you can move around easily and with confidence.

What’s the Purpose of This Website? We provide you with all the essential information about trams, buses, metros, and ferries so you can get around the city efficiently.

Key Features You Can Expect:

  • Public Transport Routes to Major Tourist Attractions: Simple, clear guides to routes connecting you to popular destinations like the Rijksmuseum, Anne Frank House, and Van Gogh Museum.
  • Ticketing Information: Learn about single-trip tickets, day passes, and multi-day options to choose the most affordable way to travel.
  • Step-by-Step Travel Guides in English :Perfect for non-Dutch speakers, ensuring you understand how to navigate the system with ease.
  • Airport to City Center Guide: Essential information for first-time visitors on the best public transport options from Schiphol Airport to downtown Amsterdam.
Who is This Website For? Our website is designed for all visitors to Amsterdam, particularly those who need reliable and easy-to-understand information about public transport. Whether you’re visiting for the first time or returning to explore more, we help ensure your journey is smooth.
