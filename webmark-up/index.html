Your one-stop resource for navigating the city’s public transportation. Whether you’re here for a short visit or an extended stay, we’ve got everything you need to travel smoothly and confidently.

What’s the Purpose of This Website?

We provide all the essential details on trams, buses, and metros to help you get around Amsterdam with ease. You’ll find everything from route maps to ticketing options, making your journey simple and stress-free.

Key Features You Can Expect

  • Transport Routes to Major Attractions: Discover easy routes to iconic spots like the Rijksmuseum, Anne Frank House, and Van Gogh Museum.
  • Ticketing Information: Find information on single-trip tickets, day passes, and multi-day options to pick the most affordable way to travel.
  • Step-by-Step Travel Guides in English: Perfect for non-Dutch speakers, our clear guides help you navigate the public transport system with ease.
  • Airport to City Center Guide: First-time visitor? Learn how to travel from Schiphol Airport to downtown Amsterdam quickly and efficiently.

Who Is This Website For?

This guide is for all Amsterdam visitors looking for clear, reliable information on public transportation. Whether you’re exploring for the first time or returning, we ensure your journey is smooth and enjoyable.
