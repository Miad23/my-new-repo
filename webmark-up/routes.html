Best Public Transport Routes to Major Tourist Attractions in Amsterdam

As a tourist, here are the easiest ways to reach Amsterdam’s top attractions using public transportation.

1. Rijksmuseum

Address: Museumstraat 1, 1071 XX Amsterdam

Transport Options:

  • Take Tram 2, 5, or 12 to the Rijksmuseum stop, which is right outside the museum.
  • Bus 397 from Schiphol Airport also stops nearby at Museumplein.
  • From Station Vondelpark, take tram 3, a short 10-minute ride to the museum.

2. Anne Frank House

Address: Westermarkt 20, 1016 GV Amsterdam

Transport Options:

  • Tram 13 or 17 to Westermarkt, a short walk from the museum.
  • Bus 170, 172, or 174 stop at Westermarkt.

Why Visit? The Anne Frank House offers an emotional journey through history. Located on the Prinsengracht canal, it's an essential stop for history lovers.

3. Van Gogh Museum

Address: Museumplein 6, 1071 DJ Amsterdam

Transport Options:

  • Tram 2, 5, or 12 stops at Museumplein, close to the Van Gogh Museum.
  • Bus 397 stops nearby.

Why Visit? The Van Gogh Museum houses the world's largest collection of Van Gogh's works, making it essential for art lovers.
